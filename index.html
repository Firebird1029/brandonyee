After Punahou School switched to remote education and an entirely new schedule system midway throughout the 2020 spring semester, Punschedule was an easy way for students to generate their new distance learning schedule digitally. Students could either allow Punschedule to automatically pull and process their schedule based on myBackpack or manually enter in their courses and pre-remote course times. After generating their schedule, students could modify and customize their new schedule, view office hours and subject due dates, as well as download their schedule into a PDF. Punschedule was used by roughly 90% of the high school student body.
